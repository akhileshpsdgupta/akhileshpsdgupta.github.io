Research

Published Research:

  • Strengthening non-engineered building using vegetal FRCM retrofitting: A numerical modeling approach for seismic assessment under the supervision of Assoc. Prof. Dr. Kshitij Charan Shrestha: Click here
    Published in Structures (Elsevier) - 1st Author
    - Numerical validation of experimental results for the moment capacity of vegetal FRCM-strengthened beams
    - Full-scale application of vegetal FRCM for retrofitting non-engineered buildings in Nepal
    - Modeling and numerical simulation of bare frames and retrofitted models
    - Eigenvalue analysis, pushover analysis, assessment of ductility reduction factors, and fragility analysis
    - Conclusion: Increase in base shear and ductility, and reduction in the vulnerability of retrofitted models
    - Tools used: DIANA 10.3, MATLAB, AutoCAD, LaTeX
